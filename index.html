Metabolic Epilepsy Assessment

Answer these questions to assess your risk factors for metabolic epilepsy triggers.

1. Do you experience jerking sensations before, during, or after playing video games?

2. After eating foods high in sugar, bread, or processed carbs, have you ever noticed fatigue, dizziness, or brain fog within a few hours?

3. Have you had a seizure during or after sleeping?

4. Have you ever had muscle twitches, jerks, or visual flashes during times of stress, exhaustion, or after overeating processed foods?

5. Do you feel mentally clearer or more focused when you avoid refined sugars or refined carbohydrates?

6. Have you noticed that artificial sweeteners such as aspartame or MSG trigger headaches, confusion, or tension

7. Have you noticed digestive issues such as bloating, irregularity, or nausea following processed meals?

8. Do you breifly lose consciousness sometimes?

9. Do you experience headaches or migraines frequently?

10. Do you have a family history of epilepsy or metabolic disorders?
